Get Involved

The Neurokernel Project is a community project that welcomes contributors from across the research spectrum. There are a number of ways to participate in the project; these include (but are not limited to): (i) submitting new Requests for Comment, (ii) providing/implementing open-source code, (iii) participating in the modeling and development discussions, (iv) exploring/evaluating the capabilities of the Neurokernel, (v) suggesting extensions to available fly brain circuits, and (vi) proposing novel fly brain circuits and architectures to code builders. We encourage you to get in touch with current modelers, developers, and users by submitting bug reports or by joining the project mailing list.
