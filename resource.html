Anatomical Data

Link Infomation
Medulla Data Medulla data published by Janelia.
Lamina Data Lamina Data published by Janelia.
Open Connectome Project All levels of “connectomes”, from the nano (using electron microscopy) to the macro (using magnetic resonance imaging).
Virtual Fly Brain An interactive tool for neurobiologists to explore the detailed neuroanatomy, neuron connectivity and gene expression of the adult Drosophila melanogaster brain.
FlyBrain An online atlas and database of the Drosophila nervous system published by ASU.
BrainBase A database hosting a large collection of confocal microscopy images showing expressions of VT and enhancer trap Gal4 lines generated by Barry Dickson and his group over several years at the Institute of Molecular Pathology Vienna.
FlyCircuit A public database for online archiving, cell type inventory, browsing, searching, analysis and 3D visualization of individual neurons in the Drosophila brain..
